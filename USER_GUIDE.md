## 🚀 Getting Started

### **System Login**
1. Open your web browser and navigate to the CityCare URL
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login" to access your dashboard

### **First-Time Login**
- Use the default credentials provided by your administrator
- Change your password immediately after first login
- Update your profile information

### **Password Requirements**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Must include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Must include at least one number
- Recommended to include special characters

## 👨‍💼 Administrator Guide

### **Dashboard Overview**
The administrator dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the entire hospital system:

**Key Metrics Displayed:**
- Total patients registered
- Total appointments today
- Revenue overview
- Staff availability
- System health indicators

### **User Management**

#### **Adding New Users**
1. Navigate to "Users" → "Add User"
2. Fill in user information:
   - Name and email
   - Phone number
   - Role assignment
   - Department (if applicable)
3. Set temporary password
4. Click "Create User"

#### **Managing User Roles**
- **Administrator**: Full system access
- **Doctor**: Patient management and consultations
- **Pharmacist**: Inventory and prescription management
- **Receptionist**: Patient registration and appointments
- **Cashier**: Billing and payment processing
- **Patient**: Personal dashboard and appointments

#### **User Permissions**
- Edit user profiles
- Reset user passwords
- Activate/deactivate accounts
- View user activity logs

### **System Configuration**

#### **Hospital Settings**
1. Go to "Settings" → "Hospital Information"
2. Update hospital details:
   - Hospital name and address
   - Contact information
   - Working hours
   - Department information

#### **Payment Methods**
1. Navigate to "Settings" → "Payment Configuration"
2. Enable/disable payment methods:
   - Cash payments
   - Mobile money (MTN, Airtel)
   - Card payments
   - Insurance billing

### **Reports and Analytics**

#### **Generating Reports**
1. Go to "Reports" section
2. Select report type:
   - Revenue reports
   - Patient statistics
   - Appointment analytics
   - Staff performance
3. Choose date range
4. Click "Generate Report"
5. Export as CSV or PDF

#### **Dashboard Analytics**
- Real-time patient flow
- Revenue trends
- Appointment patterns
- Staff utilization

## 👨‍⚕️ Doctor Guide

### **Dashboard Overview**
The doctor dashboard provides quick access to:
- Today's appointments
- Patient queue
- Recent consultations
- Prescription requests

### **Appointment Management**

#### **Viewing Appointments**
1. Navigate to "Appointments" tab
2. View appointment list with:
   - Patient name and details
   - Appointment time
   - Reason for visit
   - Status (pending, confirmed, completed)

#### **Managing Appointments**
- **Confirm**: Accept scheduled appointments
- **Start**: Begin consultation
- **Complete**: Mark consultation as finished
- **Cancel**: Cancel with reason

### **Patient Consultations**

#### **Starting a Consultation**
1. Click "Start Consultation" on patient appointment
2. Review patient medical history
3. Record vital signs:
   - Blood pressure
   - Temperature
   - Weight
   - Heart rate

#### **Recording Consultation**
1. **Chief Complaint**: Patient's main concern
2. **Diagnosis**: Medical diagnosis
3. **Treatment Plan**: Recommended treatment
4. **Prescriptions**: Generate electronic prescriptions
5. **Follow-up**: Schedule next appointment if needed

#### **Prescription Management**
1. Click "Add Prescription"
2. Select medications from inventory
3. Specify dosage and instructions
4. Add notes for pharmacist
5. Save and print prescription

### **Patient Records**

#### **Viewing Patient History**
1. Search patient by name or ID
2. Click "View Medical History"
3. Review:
   - Past consultations
   - Previous prescriptions
   - Allergies and reactions
   - Lab results

#### **Updating Patient Records**
- Edit patient demographics
- Update medical history
- Add new allergies
- Record vaccination history

## 💊 Pharmacist Guide

### **Dashboard Overview**
The pharmacist dashboard displays:
- Pending prescriptions
- Low stock alerts
- Expiry notifications
- Today's dispensing activity

### **Prescription Management**

#### **Processing Prescriptions**
1. Navigate to "Prescriptions" tab
2. View pending prescriptions:
   - Patient information
   - Doctor details
   - Medication list
   - Dosage instructions

#### **Dispensing Medications**
1. Verify prescription details
2. Check medication availability
3. Dispense prescribed quantity
4. Update inventory automatically
5. Generate dispensing receipt

#### **Prescription Status**
- **Pending**: Awaiting processing
- **Dispensed**: Given to patient
- **Partially Dispensed**: Some items unavailable
- **Cancelled**: Doctor cancelled prescription

### **Inventory Management**

#### **Stock Monitoring**
1. Go to "Inventory" section
2. View current stock levels:
   - Available quantity
   - Reorder level
   - Expiry dates
   - Supplier information

#### **Managing Stock**
- **Add Stock**: Receive new supplies
- **Update Prices**: Adjust medication prices
- **Set Alerts**: Configure low-stock notifications
- **Expiry Tracking**: Monitor expiring medications

#### **Low Stock Alerts**
- Automatic notifications for items below reorder level
- Expiry warnings for medications expiring in 30 days
- Supplier contact information for quick reordering

### **Supplier Management**

#### **Adding Suppliers**
1. Navigate to "Suppliers" → "Add Supplier"
2. Enter supplier details:
   - Company name
   - Contact information
   - Payment terms
   - Available medications

#### **Order Management**
- Create purchase orders
- Track order status
- Receive deliveries
- Update inventory on delivery

#### **Booking Appointments**

4. Select date and time:

- **Cancel**: Cancel with reason
- **Confirm**: Send confirmation to patient
- **Remind**: Send appointment reminders

#### **Managing Doctor Schedules**

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### **Common Issues**

#### **Login Problems**
- **Incorrect Password**: Use "Forgot Password" feature
- **Account Locked**: Contact administrator
- **Browser Issues**: Clear cache and cookies
- **Network Issues**: Check internet connection

#### **Appointment Issues**
- **No Available Slots**: Try different date/time
- **Doctor Not Available**: Choose different doctor
- **System Error**: Contact receptionist
- **Confirmation Not Received**: Check email/spam folder

#### **Payment Issues**
- **Payment Failed**: Try different payment method
- **Incorrect Amount**: Verify bill details
- **Receipt Not Generated**: Contact cashier
- **Insurance Claim Delayed**: Follow up with administrator

#### **Technical Issues**
- **Slow Performance**: Clear browser cache
- **Page Not Loading**: Refresh page
- **Data Not Saving**: Check internet connection
- **Error Messages**: Screenshot and report to IT

### **Getting Help**

#### **Contact Support**
- **IT Helpdesk**: ext. 1234
- **System Administrator**: admin@citycare.com
- **Training Department**: training@citycare.com

#### **User Resources**
- **Online Help**: Click "Help" button in system
- **User Manual**: Download from dashboard
- **Training Videos**: Available in learning portal
- **FAQ Section**: Common questions and answers

#### **Reporting Issues**
1. Note error message
2. Screenshot if possible
3. Describe steps to reproduce
4. Contact IT support
5. Follow up on resolution
